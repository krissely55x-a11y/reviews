Overview

100 Doors: Escape from School stacks room-by-room puzzles in a light comic setting. Each door is a self-contained brain teaser that encourages you to experiment with your mobile device's capabilities, making full use of touch, tilt, and shake mechanics.

Gameplay Mechanics

The game features pattern recognition puzzles, mathematical riddles, ciphers, and interactive object manipulation. Solutions require you to use pinch, rotate, shake, and tilt gestures. Each puzzle is designed to be logical once you identify the hint, with difficulty ramping up steadily across the 100+ levels.

What sets this game apart is its creative use of mobile device features. You might need to shake your phone to reveal hidden numbers, tilt it to pour virtual liquid, or even use your device's flashlight to illuminate dark clues.

Progression & Monetization

Levels unlock sequentially, ensuring a smooth difficulty curve. The game offers hints and level skips through optional video ads or small in-app purchases. Australian players can complete the entire campaign for free with patience and creative thinking, though the hint system is helpful for particularly challenging puzzles.

The visual design prioritizes clarity and functionality over flashy graphics. The bright, cartoon-like art style makes interface elements easily readable on both phones and tablets. Sound effects provide satisfying audio feedback for successful solutions, with optional background music that doesn't interfere with concentration.
